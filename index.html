Recipe (insp. @its.chloeh)


Ingredients

  ▪ 3 ripe bananas

  ▪ 160g caster sugar

  ▪ 20g dark brown sugar

  ▪ 1 large room temp egg

  ▪ 60g melted butter

  ▪ 205g flour

  ▪ 1tsp baking soda

  ▪ 1tsp salt

  ▪ 1/2 tsp cinnamon

  ▪ 1 tsp vanilla extract

  ▪ dried blueberries


Steps

  ▪ Peel and mash bananas into a bowl

  ▪ Combine all the ingredients and mix until just combined

  ▪ Pour the mixture into a lined loaf tin

  ▪ Bake at 160*C for 48 mins in the air fryer

  ▪ Let it cool, slice and enjoy

However, since my Twitter API authorization didn't come on time, I performed lexical filtering (using EmoLex) to filter out tweets that didn't contain emotive content.

I learned a new lesson this week, which was to read the assigned papers carefully. During our meeting, I was able to follow along easily, which never happened in my previous research projects.

One big challenge for me was to obtain the Twitter API authorization. Since that was the first time I tried to use Twitter API, I had to apply for a developer account. The authorization took several days to arrive, which significantly delayed the workflow. In the end, Alex, my co-worker, offered to help me scrape the tweets, so I was only responsible for the lexical filtering task.

Apart from working, I had the chance to explore my hometown. My close friend and I walked around Hanoi, bought a few books, and visited a famous temple (Den Ngoc Son) for the first time. Next week, I plan to stay at home to help my sister with her homework.
